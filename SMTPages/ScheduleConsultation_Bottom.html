What to Expect During a Consultation

1

MEET WITH AN EXPERT

The meeting will begin with a warm welcome and a discussion of your brand and company identity. We’ll examine your business processes, highlighting strengths and challenges and determining pain points. We’ll also cover goals so SuiteDynamics experts know how to guide your NetSuite journey.

2

DETERMINE HOW TO REACH YOUR GOALS

Our team will explain NetSuite requirements. We’ll outline the process for your NetSuite implementation, integration, customization, or training program. If your company desires specific features, we’ll discuss them during this time and determine whether you’ll need customization to achieve your goals.

3

ESTABLISH A TIMELINE

We’ll cover the next steps in your company’s digital transformation and establish a timeline for further demos and presentations. You can also schedule follow-up meetings and request additional documentation.

4

FINAL BREAKDOWN

The consultation will end after 30 minutes when your needs have been carefully heard and thoughtfully addressed.

After this meeting, we can start building a solid partnership that will help your company become better organized, more efficient, and more powerful with NetSuite.
