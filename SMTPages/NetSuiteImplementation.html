How a NetSuite Implementation Works

The implementation process involves six steps that take your company from initial meetings to go-live. During each phase,
you’ll work with the same SuiteDynamics team. Therefore, you can rest easy, knowing your project is in the hands of
people who have gotten to know your company and its needs.

IMPLEMENTATION PROCESS

1. SYSTEM DESIGN/
CONFIGURATION

The SuiteDynamics Project Team will mold a NetSuite environment to suit your processes, align with your business requirements, and facilitate your company’s future growth. Our team will deliver a system tailor-made for your business, whether you need complex approval routings, dynamic pricing, integrations with your 3PL, or any other advanced features.

2. DATA
MIGRATION

A system is useless without accurate, complete data-no matter how advanced it is. After our team has configured your environment, we’ll migrate your core accounting and business management record data from your current system into NetSuite using a CSV import. We can also provide CSV templates and CSV import templates for each type of record, including customer and vendor.

3. TESTING

We don’t leave success to chance. Before go-live, our experts will perform quality assurance testing and offer a final client validation. We’ll ensure your NetSuite system exceeds expectations and that all its features, from the dashboards to the reports, meet your needs.

4. USER
TRAINING

You can’t maximize your new ERP system if your team doesn’t know how to operate it. Before go-live, SuiteDynamics can provide user training for your entire staff. Our instructional programs include educational resources, one-on-one training, and team training. Our experts will ensure your team can navigate each part of the system and use the software flawlessly.

5. GO-LIVE

It’s the Big Day! Your system is going online. A successful transition requires strict attention to detail from our experts and close collaboration between your team and ours. Then, your company can move smoothly to your new digital environment and hit the ground running.

6. POST-GO-LIVE
SUPPORT

Every implementation needs some level of post-go-live support. Some companies want additional training, while others require further enhancements. SuiteDynamics offers various post-implementation services so your NetSuite ERP can keep boosting efficiency and productivity for years to come.
